# Code of Conduct

panops adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## The short version

Be decent. Disagree on technical merits, not on people. Assume good faith, but call out behavior that crosses the line. Harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination aren't tolerated in issues, PRs, discussions, or any other project space.

## Reporting

Email panops@tzk.ar with `[panops conduct]` in the subject. Reports stay confidential. I'll acknowledge within a week and decide on a response (warning, temporary ban, permanent ban) proportional to the incident.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces (GitHub repo, discussions, issues, PRs) and to public spaces when someone is representing the project.
